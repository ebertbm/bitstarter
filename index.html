GoTrip will help travelers to find the fittest travel according to their budget, interest and break styles.

The process of searching where to travel can be really hard, as there are many places to visit, many web pages to consult, and plenty of results to see and reviews from other travellers to read. It can take lots of time to make decision of where to go. And then, when we finally decide, we don’t even know if that travel is on budget. So, GoTrip wants to do the search process easier by giving different alternatives of destinations, all displayed in one screen, summarizing the information of flights, hotels, activities and local food. GoTrip wants to inspire travellers to discover amazing places for their next travel.

How does it work?

Imagine if a user could see a map showing various alternatives departing from his current location to different destinations worldwide. As the user interacts with the page, those alternatives will vary according to the user preferences, especially by his budget. Each one of the alternatives will have an average cost for traveling there, which will be calculated by adding flights, hotels, activities and restaurants costs.

1. Until now the user sees only default destinations from his location. Here is when the game begins. The user can narrow his budget and the map will start showing destinations within the money range.

2. What would happen if the user is focused in doing activities in his travel instead of spending money on hotels, flights or even food? Easy, the user will have the option to re-distribute his budget according to his preferences. i.e: 50% for doing activities, 20% for flights, 20% for accommodation and 10% for food (not interested at all). The search algorithm will match these criteria and will give the user the most amazing destinations that fit his search criteria and budget.

3. What if is not a single traveller? The user can choose whether it is a couple trip, a family trip or a friends trip.

4. And finally, the user can also detail his interests for more specific results. i.e. love trip, exotic animals, fancy food, extreme sports activities, arts, among others. Our destinations will have different tags provided by the sources of the information.

5. The final results: Destinations totally FITTED to the traveller’s budget and preferences, INSPIRE him to make the best travel decision.

Where the data is coming from?

GoTrip will be feed by data from affiliates travel websites as Skyscanner, Expedia, TravelAdvisor, Yelp, among others.

What about money earning?

The principal focus of GoTrip is given users the best destinations results but the idea is to provide users an easy way for booking flights, hotels and activities once they are confident with their fitted results. The booking process will be a gateway to GoTrip affiliates. Therefore, GoTrip will earn commissions by all successful hotels, activity planners and flights bookings.
