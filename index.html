GoTrip will help travelers to find the fittest travel according to their budget, interest and break styles.

Search process of where to travel is hard, even with reviews from other travelers. If a traveler is on budget, he wouldn't cares about the reviews of an expensive place. We want to do the search proccess easier giving different alternatives of destinations summarizing the information of flights, hotels, activities and local food.

How does it work?

Imagine, if a user could see a map centered in his location and showing various alternatives departing to different destinations. Each one of those alternatives will have an average cost of going there, that average cost will be calculed using the cost of flights, hotels, activities and restaurants. That data will be consulted through all the principal travel sites.

Until now the user could changes the budget of his travel either narrow down or expanding their choices of trips

What happen if the user is focused only to spend money in doing activities in his travel instead of spending money on hotels, flights or even food. Easy, the user could distribute their budget with his preferences and the search alghorithm will giving a destination according to them.

And finally, the user could details his interests. i.e. A love trip, extreme sports activities, He loves arts..etc. The final resutls: Destinations totally fitted for the traveler.

What about money earning?

Because GoTrip will be feeded by the data of travel websites affiliates. The idea is to earn commission by all succesful hotels and flights bookings through GoTrip.
